About Us

Hello!

Here at InstaBag, we strive to create the smoothest, most user-friendly place to shop for all your clothing at the click of a button!

While going out looking for clothing, we realized how difficult it was to shop outside. Hopping from store to store, looking at every individual rack, checking if it's the right size, trying to find something similar for a lower price, the list goes on! So we decided to create a simple, one-stop-shop that has an easy-to-use interface with free returns if the sizing isn't correct. Now, everyone can shop from the ease and comfort of their home!
